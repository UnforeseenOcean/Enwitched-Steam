Enwitched 1.0 Let's Pretend You're The Pro™

Type appid section (which comes after "store.steampowered.com/app/" on Store Page link, in a form of numbers) here, then press the button below.
Only numbers, please. Otherwise, Steam-Idle.exe will crash.
e.g. For The Witcher 3: Wild hunt, which has the page with link "store.steampowered.com/app/292030/", so you just have to type "292030".

Here's where the magic happens


The appid goes here! Make sure you have Steam client running and logged in.
Plus, make sure you aren't running any games on Steam while you're doing this.
This also applies to Steam Family Sharing.
You cannot play games until you close Steam-Idle!

How do I use this?

Sigh, you really don't know YET? Fine. Here's how you do it.
First, you go to whatever game you want to idle in on your Steam client.
Second, click on Store Page link.
Third, click anywhere outside the description and stuff, then press Copy Page URL.
Forth, paste what you copied on Notepad or something. Then, erase ALL but NUMBERS on the end.
Fifth, you copy-paste it on the blank given above this section.
Finally, you just press that button just below where you just pasted that number in.
After idling for as long as you want, just close this window and "You're Now In Game" window to finish. Easy-peasy.

About this tool

This is a blind frontend, which is designed for helping people idle on their games.
If you need a list of games and their appid, use online services available basically everywhere.

p.s. You cannot pretend to play games you don't own! This is NOT a Crack tool.
For example, you can't go idle in Lord of The Fallen or Borderlands 2 if you don't own it.
